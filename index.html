Morgan and Alex met the day they signed an apartment lease to live together.

Yes, you read that correctly.

Alex had lived with a friend (Leslie) during the Fall '10/Spring '11 year at Iowa State. Morgan wanted to live with her the following year (they knew each other from marching band) so the three of them went to look at an apartment together in late January 2011.

After that day, the two of them started to hang out together more in order to get to know eachother.
In fact, one time, before Morgan had saved Alex's number in her phone she accidentally double booked a 'hangout date' with Alex and a 'date-date' with someone else on the same night. (Whoops!)

A few short months later, Morgan made the first move and planted a kiss on Alex.
Now, they didn't move in together until August, so they had a few months to figure out if it wouldn't work. This approach is NEVER suggested, but it's worked for them.

Over the years Morgan and Alex have had their ups and downs.
A short stint at long-distance dating,
multiple international trips,
five and a half years,
and two dogs later and they're (finally) getting married!
