A history lesson

Morgan and Alex met when they signed a lease for an apartment together.
Yes, you read that right.
Both Alex and Morgan went to Iowa State University in Ames, Iowa.
They signed a lease with a mutual friend (Leslie) on a cold day in late January and the move-in date was set for August to correspond with the new school year.
Once the lease was signed, the started to hang out together to get to know eachother better.
It took about a month and a half before Morgan made the first move (while watching the Oscars together, something Alex probably wouldn't willingly do on his own).
It's been many years since that winter of 2011, but they're finally gettin' hitched!

Poppin' that question

Alex asked Morgan to marry him in Kew Gardens, London, UK on June 25, 2016.
It was pouring rain and they were on a bridge in the middle of lake on their first day in Europe.
Two minutes earlier, he joked that it was the perfect time to "tie his shoe". A joke he had been playing on her, pretending to get down one knee, for a while.
When he actually did get down on one knee (completely soaked his khaki pants), she asked "are you serious?"
He then said,"Well I'm not down here for no reason!"
Once Morgan pulled her head out of her butt and realized this was the real deal, she said YES!
And they promptly made a few international phone calls totaling $25.
Totally worth it!

Gettin' hitched
